02 — Fit analysis

When to choose MentionWatch

  • You want webhooks delivered to your server, not a dashboard to check manually
  • You want to integrate monitoring into your existing workflow — Slack, PagerDuty, your own app
  • You're a developer or have a developer on your team
  • Your budget is under $50/mo
  • You're building an AI agent or automation that needs to consume mentions programmatically

When not to choose MentionWatch

  • You need 50+ social media sources today — Brandwatch is the answer
  • You need a visual dashboard for a non-technical marketing team
  • You need built-in sentiment analysis (this is on our roadmap, not live)
  • You need an enterprise SLA, dedicated support, or SSO

03 — When to build your own

Seriously consider building your own if…

We're not going to pretend a paid service always beats a DIY approach. For some use cases, rolling your own is the right call.

  • You only need one source — the HN Algolia API is free, well-documented, and handles basic keyword search out of the box
  • You have specific scraping needs or access to internal data sources we don't cover
  • You want full control over polling frequency, data retention, and storage format
  • You have engineering capacity and time is not a constraint

The honest calculus: a simple poller takes 2–4 hours to build. Add retry logic, deduplication, delivery guarantees, and multi-source support and you're at 2–4 days. MentionWatch exists for the case where you'd rather ship product than plumbing.
