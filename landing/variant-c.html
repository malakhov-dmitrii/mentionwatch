FAQ

How fast are mentions delivered? +
We poll sources every 5–10 minutes. Most mentions arrive within 15 minutes.
What if my webhook endpoint is down? +
We retry 3 times with exponential backoff (10s, 60s, 5min). Failed deliveries are stored and retrievable via GET /api/mentions.
How do I verify webhooks are from MentionWatch? +
Every request includes X-MentionWatch-Signature (HMAC-SHA256). See verification docs for Node.js and Python examples.
Which sources are live right now? +
Hacker News and Reddit are live. More coming based on customer demand.

Why is this cheaper than Mention.com? +
