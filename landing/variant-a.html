FAQ

How fast do mentions arrive?

Most mentions are delivered within 15 minutes of appearing on a live source. Hacker News and Reddit are crawled continuously. Delivery time depends on crawl frequency and your endpoint's response time.

What if my webhook endpoint goes down?

We retry delivery 3 times with exponential backoff — at 5 minutes, 30 minutes, and 2 hours. If all three fail, the event is logged in your dashboard so you can replay it manually at any time.

How do I verify webhook authenticity?

Every request carries an X-MentionWatch-Signature header containing an HMAC-SHA256 digest of the raw body, signed with your secret key. Compute the same digest on your end and compare — if they match, the payload is genuine.

Which sources are live right now?

Hacker News and Reddit are live today. Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Product Hunt, Dev.to, and GitHub are in active development and will roll out over the coming months. Subscribers will get early access.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes — cancel from your dashboard in one click. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You keep access until the end of your billing period, then it stops.

Why is this cheaper than other monitoring tools?

Most monitoring tools are built for marketing teams and priced accordingly — dashboards, reports, seat licenses. MentionWatch is an API. No dashboard overhead, no per-seat pricing. You get raw data via webhooks, cheaper, and you build whatever you want on top.
