FAQ

How fast are mentions delivered? +
We poll sources every 5-10 minutes. Most mentions are delivered within 15 minutes of appearing on the source. HN stories are typically fastest, Reddit slightly slower depending on subreddit activity.
What happens if my webhook endpoint is down? +
We retry 3 times with exponential backoff (10 seconds, 60 seconds, 5 minutes). If all retries fail, the mention is stored and can be retrieved via the GET /api/mentions endpoint.
How do I verify webhooks are from MentionWatch? +
Every webhook includes an X-MentionWatch-Signature header with an HMAC-SHA256 signature. Your webhook secret is shown on your welcome page after signup. See the verification docs for code examples.
Which sources are currently live? +
Hacker News and Reddit are live now. Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Product Hunt, Dev.to, and GitHub Discussions are coming soon. We're adding sources based on customer demand — let us know which you need.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal. No questions asked. Your subscription runs until the end of the billing period.
Why is this so much cheaper than Mention.com or Brand24? +
We don't build dashboards, analytics suites, or enterprise features. We build an API. That means less code, less infrastructure, and we pass the savings to you. If you need a full analytics dashboard, those tools are great. If you just want the data via webhook, we're the better fit.
