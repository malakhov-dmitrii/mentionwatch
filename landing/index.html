FAQ

How fast are mentions delivered? +
We poll sources every 5–10 minutes. Most mentions arrive within 15 minutes.
What happens if my webhook endpoint is down? +
We retry 3 times with exponential backoff (10s, 60s, 5 min). Failed deliveries are stored and retrievable via GET /api/mentions.
How do I verify webhooks are from MentionWatch? +
Every webhook includes an X-MentionWatch-Signature header with HMAC-SHA256. See the verification docs for Node.js and Python examples.
Which sources are live? +
Hacker News and Reddit. More coming based on customer demand.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal.
Why is this cheaper than Mention.com? +
We build an API, not dashboards. Less code, less infrastructure, lower price.
