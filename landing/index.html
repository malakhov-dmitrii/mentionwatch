Frequently asked

How fast are mentions delivered? +
We poll continuously. Starter accounts see mentions within ~10 minutes; Pro accounts get priority polling at ~5 minutes. Most mentions arrive well under the maximum.
What happens if my webhook endpoint is down? +
We retry three times with exponential backoff: 10 seconds, 60 seconds, then 5 minutes. After three failures the mention is stored and retrievable via GET /api/mentions so nothing is lost.
How do I verify webhooks are genuinely from MentionWatch? +
Every delivery includes an X-MentionWatch-Signature header containing an HMAC-SHA256 hash of the body, keyed with your account secret. The verification docs include copy-paste examples for Node.js and Python.
Which sources are available today? +
Hacker News and Reddit are live now. Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Product Hunt, Dev.to, and GitHub are on the roadmap. Sources are prioritized by customer demand — tell us what you need.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime through the Stripe billing portal. You keep access until the end of the billing period. No questions asked, no retention flows.
Why is this cheaper than Mention.com or Brandwatch? +
We ship an API, not an analytics dashboard. No charts, no teams UI, no enterprise sales process. Less surface area means a lower cost basis and a significantly lower price.
